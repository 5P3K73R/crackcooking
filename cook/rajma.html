Rajma (Kidney Bean Curry)

5 min
20 min
2 people

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Rajma beans
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Jeera
  • 2 tsp Corriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Haldi (Turmeric) Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli powder
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 1/2 Tomatoes
  • 2 Green Chillies
  • 2 inch Ginger
  • 4 Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Wash the Rajma beans and soak them overnight.
  2. Put the Rajma in a pressure cooker with 2 cup water and 1 tsp Salt.
    Cook at high flame for the first whistle and 10-15 minutes.
  3. Cut the tomatoes and blend it along with Green Chillies, Ginger, and Garlic cloves to make a paste.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and add Jeera. Finely chop the Onion and fry it till light brown.
  5. Add the paste and cook it with a lid for 5 to 7 minutes at medium flame.
  6. Now add Corriander powder, Haldi powder, and Red Chilli Powder and cook the paste until it is semi dry.
  7. Add this paste back into the pressure cooker and cook for around 5 minutes.
