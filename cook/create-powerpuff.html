Experimental Concoction with Sugar, Spice, and Everything Nice

None
5 min
3 people

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Sugar (2kg)
  • 2 Jars of Spice (2 x 300g)
  • 1 Bucket of Everything Nice (5kg)
  • 1 Beaker of Chemical X

Be careful while measuring the ingredients as incorrect amounts will lead to unexpected results.

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the ingredients (except for Chemical X).
  2. Stir the contents clockwise for 2 minutes.
  3. Quickly add in the Chemical X.
  4. Recede to a safe distance of at least 6 feet. Poof!

Notes

  • Stir for exactly 2 minutes (not a second more or less).
  • The Poof! radius is about 5 feet. Hence, it is better to stand back at least 6 feet.
