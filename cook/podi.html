Podi

10 min
5 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Chana Dal
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  • 1/4 cup Sesame
  • 12 Red Chillies
  • 2 tbsp Cumin
  • Handful Curry Leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Hing (Asafoetida)

Steps

  1. Dry roast Urad Dal and Chana Dal separately till golden.
  2. Dry roast Sesame seeds till lightly brown.
  3. Dry roast Red Chillies, Curry Leaves, and Cumin on low flame.
  4. Once cool, add all in the grinder. Add salt and hing.
  5. Grind till coarse.

Notes

  • Don't grind it to a fine powder.
