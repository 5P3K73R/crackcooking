Chicken Biryani

30 min
30 min
2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 250g Chicken
  • 200g - 250g Rice
  • 3 Onions
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Kashmiri Red Chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Jhal Chilli
  • 1/4 tsp Haldi (Turmeric)
  • 1 tsp Corriander powder
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 Bayleaf
  • 1/2 inch Cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 tsp Shah Jeera
  • 2 Green Cardamom
  • 1 Black Cardamom (optional)
  • 3 Cloves
  • 3 Black Peppercorns
  • 1 Star Anise (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 3 Green Chillies
  • 2 tbsp Onion oil
  • 100ml or 1/2 cup Curd
  • Handful chopped corriander mint
  • 1 tsp Ghee

If you want to make your own Biryani Masala, check this recipe.

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and fry it in 3 tbsp oil till brown.
    Don't throw away the oil as we'll use it to cook the chicken later.
  2. S
  3. Wash the Chicken and cut it into small pieces.
  4. For the marinate, add 1 1/2 tsp Salt, both Red Chilli powder, Turmeric powder, Corriander powder, Biryani Masala, Ginger Garlic paste, 2 chopped Green Chillies, 1 tbsp Lemon Juice, 70%-80% of the Fried Onion, handful of chopped Corriander and Mint and Ghee.
  5. Mix this with the curd and keep it in the fridge for 1 to 2 hours.
  6. Now for the water, take 3 cups of water. Add the Bayleaf, Cinnamon stick, Shah Jeera, Green and Black Cardamom, Cloves, Star Anise, Black Peppercorns, 1 tbsp Salt, 1 tbsp Lemon Juice, and 1 chopped Green Chilli. Boil the water until it starts turning yellow and then keep it aside.
  7. Wash and soak the Rice in water in a separate bowl for max 1 hour. This helps to soften the Rice.
  8. To cook the chicken, heat the oil we fried the onions in and add the marinate. Cook it on high flame for 5 minutes, then cover it with a lid and cook on medium flame till the Chicken is properly cooked.
  9. If there is still water left in the pan, keep cooking until the curry dries.
  10. Take out the bigger spices (Cardamom, Cinnamon etc.) from the water and add the Rice. Cook the rice in this water till it is 70% done (takes around 7 minutes).
  11. Don't overcook the rice as it will become too soft and can break or clump. Drain the water and lay out the rice in a large dish to let it cool.
